About Us

Welcome to Tejaswee Girls Home, your trusted home away from home. Located in a safe and central neighborhood, our hostel is dedicated exclusively to providing young women with a secure, peaceful, and empowering living environment.

We believe that every girl deserves a place where she can focus on her dreams and feel truly at ease. Our hostel offers fully-furnished rooms, healthy meal plans, 24/7 security, high-speed internet, and a supportive community for students and working women alike.

At Tejaswee Girl's Home, comfort meets care. Our friendly staff, clean facilities, and regular community activities ensure a vibrant and homely experience. Whether you're here to study, work, or pursue your passions, we are here to support you every step of the way.

Join us and become part of a sisterhood that values safety, growth, and happiness.
